ABOUT US

Carrer Builder, has been established to cater to the burgeoning international demand for high quality education in the context of a rapidly globalising educational scenario.Carrer Builder is the flagship brand of the over 5 decades old pioneering group of educational institutions headquartered in Bangalore, India. The group has an enviable track record of academic excellence over 53 years, nurturing each year 15000 children over eight campuses.The school atmosphere encourages a positive attitude towards learning and engages students by inculcating in them the habit of inquiring. Their process of learning is streamlined so that they enjoy acquiring knowledge and become lifelong learners.

LIBRARY

CLASSES

Large well ventilated classrooms with windows, fans and light ensure physical comfort for learning. The huge permanent board is placed at a vantage point for viewing. Excursion

The students of Grade 1 set off on their first field trip to Chennai Equestrian Academy with buoyant spirit. On reaching the venue students settled down to have their snacks on the lush green patch.Each Section was assigned a guide who took them around the stables. Children observed the different breeds of horses, ponies and race horses which had numbers on them. Children learnt that horses just like humans have socks on their feet and a shoe that helps them to get a grip to run faster.
